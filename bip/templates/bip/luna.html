About me,

I have been working as an educational school psychologist for the better part of two decades, supporting students who face a wide range of intellectual, emotional, and behavioral challenges. This website was created with my interest in computer programming, data analysis, and school psychology. The website aims to assist practitioners in developing FBAs that will support data-driven Behavior Intervention Plans that are effective and meaningful.
